Weekly Meetings

We host weekly meeting so that students can socialize and see what projects their peers might be working on. We also dedicate time for students to get together and collaberate on projects.

Hackathons

We host events where we allow students to compete on timed coding challenges. It provides an enviornment where students can productively compete with each other while also learning from each other. The winner of the hackathon is provided a small reward by the club.

Guest Seminars

The club will often have guest speakers come in to teach certain topics or to talk about the industry. Guest speakers will touch on topics like industry trends and standards as well as opportunities.

Peer Tutoring

The club will have sessions apart from meetings to tutor club members that are enrolled in computer science courses. The tutors are comprised club members that have previously taken and passed the class with a B or higher. There will also be a dedicated tutor for CS classes El Camino Offers.

Mock Interviews

The club will host days to perform mock interviews with students. It will go over common interview questions as well as have a dedicated portion for a constructive crituque.

Movie Night

Occasionally students just need time to relax and socialize. Movie nights provide a great eazygoing method of allowing students to interact with each other.
