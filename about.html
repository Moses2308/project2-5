FAQ

The club meets once every week on Fridays.

There are no requirements to join. Anyone with or without experience is welcomed to join. The club serves as an organization to promote collaberation within students in the field of computer science but also to encourage others to try it.

There are many events that take place within the club. There will often be guest speakers in the weekly meetings. Sometimes they will be professors from the college or professionals from the industry. They come in to discuss certain computer science topics or talk about the industry. We also hold events like hackathons so that students can have the opportunity to create projects with their peers.

Yes! The club has a discord server for students to join. There, you can work on projects with other students or even just relax and play games with your peers.

The club meets on campus in the math building. The meeting is held in room 305 at 3:00pm.
